About

A bit about me

I'm a cybersecurity analyst drawn to the offensive side of security — red teaming, penetration testing, and adversary simulation. I believe the best way to build solid defenses is to think like the attacker first. My work spans both sides: crafting attack chains and then analyzing them from the blue team perspective to understand what was missed, what was caught, and why. I document everything — because understanding the full picture matters.
